Profile Verification Process

At DOXA, we take verification seriously to ensure the quality and reliability of our engineer profiles. Our multi-step process includes:

Engineers must provide valid identification to confirm their identity.

Credential Verification

How Our Matching Algorithm Works

At DOXA, we've developed a sophisticated matching algorithm that goes beyond simple keyword matching to create meaningful connections between engineers and startups.

Skills Analysis

Our algorithm analyzes the specific skills required for each project and matches them with engineers who have verified expertise in those areas. We consider both primary and secondary skills to ensure a comprehensive match.

Project Type Compatibility

We categorize projects by type, complexity, and industry, then match them with engineers who have relevant experience in similar projects. This ensures that engineers understand the unique challenges of each project type.

Availability Assessment

Our system takes into account the availability of engineers and the timeline of each project. We only match engineers who have confirmed availability for the duration of the project, ensuring timely delivery.

Cultural Fit Evaluation

Beyond technical skills, we consider work style preferences, communication patterns, and cultural fit to ensure that engineers integrate seamlessly with startup teams.

Continuous Learning

Our algorithm learns from each successful match and project completion, continuously improving its recommendations based on feedback and outcomes.
